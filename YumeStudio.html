What's Anime?

Anime is animation from Japan drawn by hand or using computer technology. The word anime is an abbreviation of animation in English

What's Studio Anime?

An animation studio is a company producing animated media. The broadest such companies conceive of products to produce, own the physical equipment for production, employ operators for that equipment, and hold a major stake in the sales or rentals of the media produced.

About YUME Studio

YUME Studio is an anime studio originating from Indonesia, and created by @Xyccn3 or Shion Kazumi with the aim of showing the work of the nation's children
